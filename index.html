An Introduction To The Runes

Runes are an ancient Germanic alphabet, used for writing, divination and magick. They were used throughout northern Europe, Scandinavia, the British Isles, and Iceland from about 100 B.C.E. to 1600 C.E. Runic inscriptions of great age have even been found in North America, supporting stories that the Vikings arrived in the Americas long before Columbus.

Ask The Runes Your Question

You and ask the Runes your own question

Choose a subject you wish to query the runes about. It can be as specific or general as you like. Obviously it should be something of concern to you.

You will see a Rune for each the Past, the Present and the Future. Pay attention to the direction of the stone. Some Runes can be upside down (reversed) and it will change the meaning for your draw significantly. Finally, consider how the Runes are connected in this and how the meaning of each relates to the others.
